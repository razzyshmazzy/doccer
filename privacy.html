Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Doccer is a browser-based tool that connects to your Google account to list and export your Google Docs as a single PDF. This page explains what data is accessed and how it is handled.

What we access

  • Your Google account email address (to display in the app)
  • The names and creation dates of Google Docs files you own
  • The content of those files, solely to export them as a PDF

What we do not do

  • We do not store any of your data — everything stays in your browser tab
  • We do not transmit your files or account information to any server
  • We do not share, sell, or use your data for any purpose beyond generating the PDF
  • All data is discarded when you close or refresh the tab

Google API access

Doccer uses the Google Drive API with read-only access (drive.readonly) to retrieve your documents. It also requests your basic profile and email (email, profile) to display your account in the app. No write operations are performed.

Third-party services

Doccer loads the following scripts from third-party CDNs to function:

  • Google Identity Services (accounts.google.com) — for authentication
  • pdf-lib (cdn.jsdelivr.net) for PDF generation in the browser

No other third-party services are used.
